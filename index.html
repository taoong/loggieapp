About Loggie

Loggie is a mobile app designed to allow users to record and share their experiences with ease. Whether you are a full-time blogger or a casual travel hobbyist, Loggie will streamline your recording process like never before. From compiling snapshots and notes to editing photos of your experiences, Loggie does it all.

Features

  • Share by Album

    Share photo galleries based on your experiences (holiday, graduation, wedding)

  • Go Live

    Use the Live function during the occasion to keep your followers updated.

  • Write a Blog

    Enter blog posts to share your experiences, be it on a day to day basis, or in its totality.
